Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: What are your operating hours?

A: We are open Monday to Saturday, 8 am to 5:30 pm.

Q: Do you offer walk-in services?

A: Yes, we do offer walk-in services, but we recommend booking an appointment to ensure availability.

Booking and Appointments

Q: How can I book an appointment?

A: You can book an appointment online through our Booking Page, by phone at +675 7109 5910, or in person at our location.

Q: What is your cancellation policy?

A: Please provide at least 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule an appointment. Failure to do so may result in a cancellation fee.

Services and Pricing

Q: What services do you offer?

A: We offer haircuts, shaves, beard trims, and grooming packages. Detailed descriptions of our services can be found on our Services Page.

Q: How much do your services cost?

A: Our prices are listed on our Services Page and are subject to change without notice.

Other Questions

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept cash, credit, and debit cards.

Q: How can I provide feedback?

A: We value your feedback. Please email us at hausman@gmail.com with your comments and suggestions.
