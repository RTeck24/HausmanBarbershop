Hausman Barbershop Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Hausman Barbershop collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information: Name, contact details, appointment history.
Non-Personal Information: Browser type, IP address, and usage data.

3. Use of Information

To provide and manage our services. To communicate with you about appointments and promotions. To improve our website and services.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or with your consent.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy
